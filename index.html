Beats, Choices, & Neon Dreams

A Producer's Journey

The Spark

I started making music when I was in 11th grade. At that time I didn't know anything about how to make music and just like everything I decided to teach myself... I stumbled upon Ableton that stuck with me purely out of its ability to have vibrant colors...

What If #1: The DAW Decision

I might have picked a different DAW like FL Studio or Logic. Maybe the workflow wouldn't have clicked the same way... Or, who knows, maybe another interface would have unlocked a different style altogether! Ableton's visual appeal definitely helped me get comfortable quickly.

I started experimenting with the sample packs... My first computer was my iMac from mid 2010s... I found distrokid... I met my classmate who was new to the school...

What If #2: The Solo Start

If I'd released RAGE completely solo first, he might have seen its initial buzz and then decided to jump on board... Our collaboration might have taken a different shape...

RAGE

The album was hard hitting beats, no vocals, and he came up with two rock and roll instrumentals... That Album was Called RAGE. It was officially out on March 4th, 2022. That album actually did pretty well... hundreds for our first album ever... Our friends were ecstatic, our families got to know about this...

HEARTBREAK & The Whoopty Phenomenon

I sampled the vocal chop used in Whoopty... we both made our versions. He came up with an amazing beat and we decided to go for his. It was named Whoopty - Remix... That song went on to become the most streamed song... with over 500k plays on Spotify and over a million on Youtube! We were shocked. We were committed and inspired.

What If #4: No Whoopty Remix?

Without 'Whoopty - Remix,' HEARTBREAK would have been a very different album... Our growth would have been much slower...

What If #5: No Friend/Collaborator?

No, definitely not. Without him, I'd probably still be making music, but I'd likely be stuck in the tens or hundreds of plays...

Setbacks & Humility

After this I released a single called LIEz on April 30, 2022... that didn't do well. I admit I got carried away... Then we sampled The Last of us theme and that went well... I also released a song later that was taken down due to copyright called LIVING LIFE IN THE NIGHT.

What If #6: Losing Motivation After LIEz?

If LIEz had completely crushed my motivation, I might have stopped producing for a while... It would have been a significant setback...

The Grind & Shifting Tides

After that I got busy and released less often but still thrilled to release any way. Here are the rest of the releases...

The Pause & The Promise

I stopped releasing after DRAGON BALL. cause I just lost the motivation, I was tired and I had less free time... I have 3500 monthly listeners on spotify, 500k plays of WHOOPTY Remix and over a million on Youtube... But I planned to release more in the future... I promised myself to never give up...

What If #7: Not Continuing After May 2024?

If that thought to continue... wasn't there? I would probably have let it all fade... This 'NEON DREAMS' album cover wouldn't exist...

NEON DREAMS

I decided to come back stronger when I have time, when I feel better. I promised to never stop. "NEON DREAMS" is the upcoming project, a testament to that promise.

The journey continues...
